About Us

Kiavi Kreations is a small business dedicated to crafting personalized and unique gifts for every occasion. From the heartfelt to the humorous, we specialize in turning your ideas into tangible keepsakes. Our product range includes everything from custom printed glassware and ceramics to personalized stationery, 3D greeting cards, and resin creations. We also offer a variety of services such as engraving, sticker making, and handmade papercraft to cater to your specific needs.


Our goal is to create items that not only look beautiful but also hold special meaning. Whether you're celebrating a wedding, baby shower, birthday, or corporate event, we have something to make your occasion truly memorable.


Kiavi Kreations is owned and operated by Av-Vienna Bartholomew, a single mother with a passion for crafting. Despite facing the challenges of raising a son with autism and other medical conditions, Av-Vienna finds joy and purpose in her creative endeavors. Her love for crafting is evident in the care and attention to detail she puts into every piece.


At Kiavi Kreations, we believe that every gift should tell a story. Let us help you create yours.
